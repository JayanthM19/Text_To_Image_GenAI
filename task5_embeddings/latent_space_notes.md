# Latent Space Analysis

## Objective
Study compressed image representations used in Stable Diffusion.

## Key Concepts
- Variational Autoencoder (VAE)
- Latent vectors
- Image compression
- Semantic representations
- Latent interpolation

## Key Learnings
- Stable Diffusion operates in latent space
- VAE compresses images into semantic representations
- Latent interpolation enables smooth semantic transitions
