# Diffusion Schedulers in Stable Diffusion

## What is a Scheduler?
A scheduler controls how noise is removed during image generation.

It defines:
- timestep progression
- denoising intensity
- sampling behavior

---

## Why Schedulers Matter
Schedulers affect:
- image quality
- generation speed
- prompt consistency
- creativity
- stability

---

## Common Schedulers

### Euler Ancestral
Fast and creative generation.

### DDIM
Stable and deterministic generation.

### DPM Solver
Efficient high-quality generation.

### LMS
Smooth multistep denoising approach.
